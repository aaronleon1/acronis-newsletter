Fast-evolving cyberthreats are the other "new normal"

Autumn has brought a chill to the northern hemisphere, while spring is warming the southern. As the calendar keeps moving, life keeps going and change is – as the saying goes – inevitable. Or is it?

One thing has not changed this year has been the ongoing lockdown as we combat the COVID-19 pandemic. Unlike anything in our lifetime, the health crisis is affecting the way we live and work. And because we rely on data more than ever, it’s also impacting our digital lives.

The threats that have emerged during the pandemic continue to evolve, so this month’s newsletter shares the latest updates and a deep-dive into the experiences of remote workers, as well as expert advice from the recent Acronis Global Cyber Summit 2020.
